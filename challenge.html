FAQ

What is the goal of this program ?

The goal is to help individuals attain a high level of fitness through special workout programs that suits you

How much weight will I lose on this program

These special programs have been designed to make an individual lose between 3-4kg per week.

What if I find the exercise too easy or too hard ?

If you find the exercises too hard to perform, then you can reach our to our training coaches on any of our social media platforms for more advice. If the exercises are too easy, then you can upgrade the exercises to a higher level.

What should I eat during this program?

The kind of foods to eat is dependent on your goal and the exercises you do. Along sides the exercises are foods to be eaten in order for the exercise to be efficient.

It's too hard! Can I split the workout up or skip some videos ?

You can split the workout to make it easier but do not skip the videos as they are all essential.
